What is TileHub?

Tilehub is a platform independent collaborative editor for 2-dimensional maps based on graphics objects known as tilemaps. Users work on projects that contain tilemaps and provide graphics objects. The created tilemaps can be exported in various memory formats supported by video game engines. Developer teams are able to work together in real time on their tilemaps and can export them into various engines. This saves time in communication and allows users to give feedback quick and easy. It is possible to save the projects online or offline.
